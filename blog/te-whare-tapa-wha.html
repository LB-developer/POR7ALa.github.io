Personal Wellbeing

The following is a plan/commitment to guide personal wellbeing, incorporating the different aspects of Te Whare Tapa Wha.

Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing)

Going for a walk every weekday.

Taha Whānau (Family Wellbeing)

Talk with family about progress each week, how things are going, am I where I want to be, what do I need to do to get there.

Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing)

Going for a walk every weekday.
Practice mindfulness.

Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing)

Practice mindfulness.
Reflect after completing tasks.

Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment)

Walk along the beach 2/5 days a week
